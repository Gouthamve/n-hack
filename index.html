This is η-hack.

η-hack is a free 36 hour hackathon, organised by ηvision - IIT Hyderabad's techfest. Our dream has always been to organise the best tech events in Hyderabad, and we plan on living upto that dream. With that in mind, we are launching η-Hack, a pure technology hackathon. This is a hackathon where you use the latest and the greatest tools to build demoes that make the audience go "WOW!"

The hackathon spans across the third weekend of December - the 17th and 18th - at Collab House, near Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad.The best hacks in each domain will recieve prizes!

We invite everyone to apply, but we have limited space. We can't accomodate everyone, even though we want to. Hence, we encourage you to indicate your areas of interest and previous work in the field while applying.
